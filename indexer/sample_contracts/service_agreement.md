ARTICLE 1. PARTIES AND DEFINITIONS

1.1 This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Contoso Technology Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales (Company Number 09876543), with its registered office at 1 Innovation Square, London, EC2A 4PP ("Provider"), and **Acme Corporation Ltd**, a company incorporated in England and Wales (Company Number 01234567), with its registered office at 50 Business Park, Manchester, M1 2AB ("Client").

1.2 "Services" means the software development, cloud hosting, and technical support services described in Schedule A attached hereto.

1.3 "Deliverables" means all software code, documentation, reports, designs, and other work product created by Provider specifically for Client under this Agreement.

1.4 "Intellectual Property Rights" means patents, copyrights, trade marks, database rights, trade secrets, and all other intellectual property rights of any kind, whether registered or unregistered.

1.6 "Effective Date" means 1 March 2026.

---

ARTICLE 2. SERVICES

2.1 Provider shall deliver the following categories of services to Client during the term of this Agreement:

2.2 **Software Development:** Provider shall develop and maintain bespoke software applications as specified in the project briefs agreed by both parties in writing. All development work shall follow industry-standard agile methodologies with fortnightly sprint reviews.

2.3 **Cloud Hosting:** Provider shall host Client's applications on Microsoft Azure infrastructure, ensuring a minimum environment of two availability zones. Provider shall conduct regular security patching, infrastructure scaling, and backup operations on Client's behalf.

2.4 **Technical Support:** Provider shall provide a dedicated support team available Monday to Friday, 08:00–18:00 GMT/BST, with on-call escalation support available 24 hours per day, 7 days per week for Priority 1 incidents as defined in Article 4.

2.5 Provider shall assign a named Client Success Manager who will attend monthly service review meetings and produce quarterly performance reports.

2.6 Client shall provide Provider with all reasonable access, data, and cooperation necessary to deliver the Services.

---

ARTICLE 3. PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Client shall pay Provider a monthly retainer of **£15,000 (fifteen thousand pounds sterling)** exclusive of VAT, invoiced on the first Business Day of each calendar month.

3.2 All invoices are due and payable within **30 days** of the invoice date.

3.3 Any undisputed invoice amounts not paid by the due date shall accrue interest at the rate of **1.5% per month** (or part thereof), calculated on a daily basis from the due date until the date of actual payment.

3.4 Client shall reimburse Provider for all reasonable pre-approved expenses incurred in connection with the Services, including travel, accommodation, and specialist third-party tooling.

3.5 Provider may suspend Services upon 7 days' written notice if Client fails to pay any undisputed amount that is more than 45 days overdue.

3.6 All fees are exclusive of Value Added Tax. VAT shall be applied at the prevailing rate and is the sole responsibility of Client.

---

ARTICLE 4. SERVICE LEVEL COMMITMENTS

4.1 Provider shall achieve a monthly uptime availability of **99.5%** for all production-hosted systems, measured on a rolling 30-day basis, excluding scheduled maintenance windows communicated at least 48 hours in advance.

4.2 Incident response times are classified as follows:

- **Priority 1 (P1) — Critical:** System unavailable or data loss risk. Initial response within **4 hours**; resolution target within **8 hours**.
- **Priority 2 (P2) — High:** Major feature unavailable with no workaround. Response within **8 Business Hours**; resolution target within **2 Business Days**.
- **Priority 3 (P3) — Medium:** Non-critical issue with workaround available. Response within **2 Business Days**; resolution target within **5 Business Days**.

4.3 Provider shall provide Client with a monthly Service Level Report within 5 Business Days of month end, detailing uptime metrics, incident counts, and resolution statistics.

4.4 Where Provider fails to meet the 99.5% uptime SLA in any calendar month, Client shall be entitled to a service credit of 5% of the monthly fee for each full percentage point below the target, up to a maximum of 20% of the monthly fee.

ARTICLE 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Each party ("Receiving Party") agrees to keep confidential all non-public information disclosed by the other party ("Disclosing Party") that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information ("Confidential Information").

6.2 The Receiving Party shall: (a) use Confidential Information solely to perform its obligations under this Agreement; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent of the Disclosing Party; (c) apply the same degree of care to protect the Confidential Information as it applies to its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

6.3 Confidentiality obligations under this Article 6 shall survive for a period of **5 years** following the termination or expiry of this Agreement.

6.4 The obligations in clause 6.2 shall not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party; or (d) is required to be disclosed by applicable law or court order, provided that the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party where permitted.

7.3 Nothing in this Agreement shall limit or exclude either party's liability for: (a) death or personal injury caused by negligence; (b) fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation; (c) any other liability which cannot be excluded by applicable law.

ARTICLE 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of **12 months** ("Initial Term"), unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article 8.

8.2 Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party gives the other at least **30 days' written notice** of its intention not to renew, prior to the end of the then-current term.

8.3 Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party: (a) commits a material breach of this Agreement and, where such breach is capable of remedy, fails to remedy it within 14 days of written notice; (b) becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or enters administration or liquidation proceedings.

8.4 Upon termination, Client shall pay all outstanding fees for Services delivered up to the termination date. Provider shall deliver all Deliverables completed to date within 15 Business Days.
